About

This website is about confessing your thoughts to someone of your own class or to give any feedback or suggestions to the class in general. The name of the sender will completely remain anonymous. Each student will have an own account where he or she will recieve messages.
The main objective of this website is to help students express themselves and take part in the betterment of the class.
